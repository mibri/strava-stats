How to get your Strava export

  1. On Strava, open Settings → My Account.
  2. Scroll to Download or Delete Your Account and click Get Started.
  3. Under Download Request (optional), click Request Your Archive. (You don't need the delete steps.)
  4. Strava emails you a export_*.zip — usually within a few hours.
  5. Download it and drop it above. Everything is processed in your browser — nothing is uploaded.
